salesEQUITY

salesEQUITY is a "Client Engagement Platform". It analyzes surveys taken by the user's clients with a patented algorithm to check the status of the relationship. This helps identify areas that need growth, or inversely, areas where the user is undervaluing themselves! Check them out here .

During my time at salesEQUITY, I practiced strict TDD to develop new features or refactor older code, AGILE programming methodology to set goals and collaborate with other developers, and pair-programming to teach and learn new skills. Sometimes at the same time!

Working on a small team of just three developers allowed me to take on a plethora of different projects - new features, refactoring, UX Design, etc. The largest of which was Trusted Advisor Action Plans which was built for clients who wanted to do more than just analyze data, but actually find solutions. The module was not only built in a timely manner, but added substaintila value to the product because now our users could actually take action to improve their relationships, not just measure and analyze them.

salesEQUITY's development stack as of writing is Ruby on Rails on the back end while using heavily Object Oriented JavaScript/jQuery on the front end. Rspec, FactoryGirl, and Capybara are the testing libraries and this is all run on a PostgreSQL database. Finally, the app is hosted on Heroku, with AWS being used for storage purposes.
